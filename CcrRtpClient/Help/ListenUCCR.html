Listen to Cork Campus Radio 97.4 fm

Figure 1.1 - Main Window of 'Cork Campus Radio RTP Client'.

To Listen to the 'Cork Campus Radio' live, Real Transport Protocol Transmission:

  • Press the 'CCR Radio' Checkbox.

  • Hit the 'Start Receiver' Button.

 



Listen to an RTP Stream using your own Paramters

Figure 1.2 - User Specified 'RTP Session Window'.

To Listen an RTP session using your own parameters: live Real Transport Protocol Transmission:

  • Press the 'Other RTP' Checkbox on the main application window(See fig. 1.1 above).

  • Enter parameters in the 'RTP Session' Window that appears (fig. 1.2 ) and click the 'OK' button at the bottom of this Window.

  • Press the 'Start Receiver' button on the main application window(See fig. 1.1 above).
  • To clear the text fields in the window, click on 'Clear Fields' Button.
